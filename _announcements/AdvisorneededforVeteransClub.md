<div><b>Body:</b> <div class="ExternalClass941A8728F97B4D3D83B38E6C3F798FCD"><div><br />The Veterans Club advisor position is currently open, and the Office of Student Life is seeking candidates. </div>
<div> </div>

<div> </div>
