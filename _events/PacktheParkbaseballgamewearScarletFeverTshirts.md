<div><b>Title:</b> &#39;Pack the Park&#39; baseball game--wear Scarlet Fever T-shirts!</div>
<div><b>Location:</b> KCC baseball diamond</div>
<div><b>Start Time:</b> 4/24/2014 2:00 PM</div>
<div><b>End Time:</b> 4/24/2014 5:00 PM</div>
<div><b>End Date:</b> 4/24/2014 1:00 PM</div>
<div><b>Created:</b> 4/18/2014 1:01 PM</div>

<div><b>KCC Description:</b> <div class="ExternalClass10847EFA984B45FA9240404982094A38"><div> </div>
<div>Fans are invited to &quot;Pack the Park&quot; Thursday, April 24 at 2 p.m. for the KCC Cavaliers men's baseball game vs. Illinois Valley Community College.
<div></div>
<div> </div>
<div>Admission is free. There will be free hot dogs, chips and lemonade for the first 100 fans.<br /><br />KCC also has Scarlet Fever T-shirts available for students and fans to wear. The shirts are $5 each, and available for purchase at the KCC information desk beginning April 21. Staff members who wear a Scarlet Fever T-shirt can wear jeans to work on Thursday.</div>
<div> </div>
<div></div>
<div>As of April 17, the Cavaliers baseball has a record of 27 wins and 12 losses. They are ranked no. 10 in the nation in NJCAA Division II. </div>
<div></div>
